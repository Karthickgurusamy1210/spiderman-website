About Spider-Man

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, gained his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He possesses incredible strength, agility, and spider-like abilities, including web-shooting and wall-crawling. As a beloved Marvel character, Spider-Man teaches us that “With great power comes great responsibility.”

Explore the Spider-Verse

The Spider-Verse is a multiverse of spider-heroes! Here are some key characters:

  • Miles Morales: A teen hero with unique powers like invisibility and venom strike.
  • Gwen Stacy (Spider-Gwen): A drummer turned superhero in her dimension.
  • Spider-Man 2099: A futuristic Spidey named Miguel O’Hara with claws and talons.
